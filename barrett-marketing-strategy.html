Why Buyers Should Tell Their Stories

This is the differentiator. Here's what you tell them:

  • Find your home faster – Realtors see you as a real person, not just another offer. Your network might know of unlisted properties. Creates momentum.
  • Build your future network – Document your journey. Your friends and family get invested. You become a resource for others later.
  • Free professional content – We create beautiful content celebrating your milestone that you can share with everyone you know.
  • Help others like you – First-time buyers inspire first-time buyers. You normalize the process and reduce fear for someone else.
  • Better service for you – Public accountability keeps everyone engaged and working harder. Your realtor gets exposure. Everyone's invested in your success.

The Buyer Story Framework

Phase 1: The Beginning

Intro post, coffee meeting, why they're buying, their hopes. Make them relatable.

Phase 2: The Journey

Preparation, education, challenges, realtor collabs. Show the real process—the hard parts and the breakthroughs.

Phase 3: The Victory

Keys in hand (yes, the traditional shot), but also Barrett + Buyer + Realtor together. Reflections on what was learned.

Phase 4: The Follow-Up

30-day, 90-day check-ins. How they're settling in. Continued relationship. Show you don't disappear after closing.
