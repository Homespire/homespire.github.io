Our Story

Built on Trust.
Driven by Purpose.

Nick Stacy started in the mortgage industry with a straightforward belief: buying a home should feel like an achievement, not an ordeal. After years of watching people get lost in paperwork and jargon, he set out to build something different — a team where every client gets a real advocate, not just a loan number.

Together with Austyn Phillips and the rest of the team, Nick built a practice rooted in Dayton, Ohio — one that combines the resources of a national lender with the personal attention of your neighbor down the street. We know your name. We know your goals. And we stay with you long after closing day.

The rhino is more than a brand mark — it's our ethos. Charge forward, stay resilient, and never stop for anything less than the keys in your hand.
